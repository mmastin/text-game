Starting up

Once you enter the dungeon, look at the first message in the chatroom
The first message contains your player id. Enter it into the player id box

Playing the game

Everyone starts with 100 health and 20 strength
You can move by inputting n, s, e, w into the direction box
You can send messages to the other players in the message box
When you move into a location with another player, they lose health
equal to your strength. Once your health reaches 0 you will lose.
You will notice that objects randomly spawn. They will either buff or
debuff your character. Have fun!
